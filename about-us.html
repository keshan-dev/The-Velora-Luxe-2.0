Our Story

The Velora Luxe

Rising with elegance along the coastal skyline, The Velora Luxe is a symbol of refined luxury and modern sophistication. Overlooking breathtaking city views, our hotel stands as the crown jewel of the waterfront, offering a serene escape at the heart of the vibrant cultural and design district. More than a destination, it’s where timeless elegance meets contemporary experience.

Velora Impact

Purpose-Driven Hospitality

At The Velora Luxe, we believe true luxury goes hand in hand with responsibility. Guided by a vision to uplift both people and the planet, we are dedicated to creating a positive impact across every aspect of our hotel experience.

Our sustainability journey, Velora Impact, is built on two core pillars: Velora Empowers, which focuses on nurturing communities and supporting local talent, and Velora Sustains, our pledge to eco-conscious practices and circular hospitality for a better tomorrow.
